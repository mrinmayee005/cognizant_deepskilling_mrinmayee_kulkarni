Allow location access to find community events happening in your area. We use high-accuracy GPS positioning for the best results.

How It Works

This feature uses the HTML5 Geolocation API to determine your current location. Here's what happens:

  • Your browser will ask for permission to access your location
  • We use high accuracy mode for precise positioning
  • A timeout of 10 seconds ensures the app doesn't hang
  • Maximum age of 5 minutes ensures fresh location data
  • Your location is never stored or transmitted - it stays on your device
