Exercise 12.2 - Spacing Utilities

Margin and Padding Utilities

Margin Utilities (m-*)

m-0 — No margin on all sides
m-3 — Small margin on all sides
m-5 — Large margin on all sides
mt-4 — Medium margin-top only
mb-5 — Large margin-bottom only
mx-auto — Horizontally centered (auto margins)
ms-auto — Pushed to the right

Padding Utilities (p-*)

p-0 — No padding
p-2 — Small padding
p-3 — Medium padding
p-5 — Large padding
py-5 — Large vertical padding (top and bottom)
px-4 — Medium horizontal padding (left and right)
pt-3 pb-1 — Different padding top and bottom

Combined Spacing in Layout

p-4 m-2
Large padding, small margin
p-2 m-4
Small padding, large margin
py-5 px-3 my-2 mx-4
Mixed axis spacing
